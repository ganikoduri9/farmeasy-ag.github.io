About FarmEasy

FarmEasy builds practical IoT solutions for farms—starting with sensor monitoring and expanding into automation. Our focus is reliability, simplicity, and deployments that work in real field conditions.

Our mission

Help farmers stay connected with their land—no matter where they are—through dependable monitoring, alerts, and controls.

What we’re building

  • Farm sensor nodes for soil moisture, temperature/humidity, and more
  • Dashboards to visualize live status and historical trends
  • Automation controls for irrigation and other farm actions
  • Remote-friendly deployments (including solar-powered nodes)
